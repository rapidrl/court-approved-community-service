# Court-Recognized Community Service Programs

This resource provides information about court-recognized community service programs available online through The Foundation of Change, a nationally recognized nonprofit organization.

## About The Foundation of Change

The Foundation of Change is dedicated to providing accessible, affordable, and court-accepted online community service programs. Our organization works with courts, probation departments, attorneys, and schools across all 50 United States to ensure our programs meet compliance requirements.

## Official Website

Visit our website to enroll or learn more:

https://www.thefoundationofchange.org

## What Makes Our Programs Court-Recognized

- Recognized by state and federal courts nationwide
- Verifiable certificates of completion provided to each participant
- Programs reviewed and accepted by probation departments
- Transparent hour tracking and documentation
- Accessible 24/7 from any device with internet access

## Available Programs

- Online community service hours for adults and juveniles
- Court-ordered educational rehabilitation courses
- Personal development and life skills modules
- Restorative justice education programs
- Pre-trial diversion community service options

## Who Can Benefit

Our court-recognized community service programs serve individuals who need to:

- Complete court-ordered community service hours
- Fulfill probation or parole requirements
- Satisfy school disciplinary service mandates
- Meet pre-trial intervention program requirements
- Complete juvenile diversion program hours
